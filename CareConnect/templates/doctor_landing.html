Is patient data fully secure and HIPAA-compliant?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II audited annually, and our Business Associate Agreement is available instantly for any plan.
How long does setup actually take?
+
Most physicians are live within 5–8 minutes. Our AI reads your NPI data to pre-populate credentials, specialties, and hospital affiliations. You review, add a photo, and publish.
Can I update my information independently?
+
Absolutely. You have 24/7 access to a simple admin dashboard to change hours, services, insurance plans, and team members. Changes go live in under 60 seconds.
What does it cost? Is there a free trial?
+
Full-featured 14-day free trial — no credit card required. Plans start at $49/month for solo practitioners and $149/month for group practices. No setup fees, no long-term contracts.
Does it integrate with my EHR or scheduling system?
+
Yes — we integrate natively with Epic, Athenahealth, Kareo, DrChrono, and 40+ systems. For systems not on our list, our team builds the connection at no additional charge.
